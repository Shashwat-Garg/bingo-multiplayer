About BINGO game

  • The game rules are staright-forward. You need to make a 5x5 matrix containg numbers between 1 ans 25. Same is done by your opponent.
  • Then game starts. Each player, in their respective turns, chooses a number from their respective matrices and marks it as crossed.
  • Once a row/column/diagonal of any player is completed, a letter from the word BINGO of that player is cut.
  • The player with all the letters of BINGO cut first is the winner. If both the players cut all the letters simultaniously, it is a draw.
